Key Features of RSQUARE

Explore the functionalities that make RSQUARE a game-changer in grade re-evaluation.

  • Automated Grade Recalculation

    RSQUARE automates the process of recalculating grades after re-evaluations, eliminating the need for manual adjustments and reducing human error. This ensures that grades are updated accurately and efficiently, freeing up valuable time for instructors.

  • Fair and Transparent Adjustments

    RSQUARE uses a percentile-based system to adjust grades fairly. This means that all students' grades are recalculated relative to the new highest score, ensuring consistency and transparency in the grading process.

  • Improved Communication and Notification

    RSQUARE provides a clear and accessible platform for students and faculty to view grade changes. Students receive notifications when their grades are updated, providing them with timely information about their academic performance.

  • Streamlined Re-evaluation Workflow

    RSQUARE simplifies the re-evaluation process, allowing students to submit requests online and providing instructors with a centralized system to review and approve them. This streamlined workflow reduces administrative burden and ensures timely grade adjustments.

  • Secure and Robust Platform

    Built with the Django framework, RSQUARE offers a secure and reliable platform for managing sensitive student data. The system is designed to ensure data integrity and protect student privacy.
